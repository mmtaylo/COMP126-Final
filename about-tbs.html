About Tau Beta Sigma and Iota Lambda

What is Tau Beta Sigma?

Tau Beta Sigma (TBS) operates as a student service and leadership recognition society whose chief aim is to assist the Director of Bands in developing the leadership and enthusiasm that they require of their band. Our goals are not only to provide the band with organized and concentrated service activities, but to give our membership valid and wholesome experiences in organization, leadership, and social contacts. The honorary nature of membership is based on our premise that "it is an honor to be selected to serve".

National Mission Statement

"We provide exceptional service to collegiate bands and promote equality and diversity, including empowering women in the band profession. We cultivate leadership, educational achievement, music appreciation and community development."

Iota Lambda

"Da ILlest" Iota Lambda chapter of Tau Beta Sigma was founded on April 19, 2008 at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. We are the 227th chapter of TBS and live within the Southeast District.

Big Sister Chapter: Epsilon Alpha - University of South Carolina
Chapter Motto: "Sisters in Harmony"
Chapter Color: Platinum Silver

Chapter Mission Statement

"Tau Beta Sigma, National Honorary Band Sorority , provides service to collegiate bands, encourages the advancement of women in the band profession, and promotes and enriches an appreciation of band music through recognition, leadership development, and education of its members."

Iota Lambda, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
