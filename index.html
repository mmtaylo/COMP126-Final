Welcome to the official website of "Da ILlest" Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma National Honorary Band Sorority at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. To find out more about Tau Beta Sigma's purposes, its history, and our very own Iota Lambda chapter, please visit our "About" section!


Under "Our Members" tab, we have posted up-to-date lists of all Iota Lambda members, our current active members, and the executive board. Our "Newsletter" tab has recent events, updated on a monthly basis!


You are welcome to contact us through the "Contact" page. Other websites related to Tau Beta Sigma and the Iota Lambda chapter are listed under "Related Websites." We hope you have a great day!
